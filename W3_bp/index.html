Foundation

BLACKPINK in your area!

On June 29, 2016, YG Entertainment announced the launch of a new girl group, marking the birth of a girl group following 2NE1 after 7 years. It was not until August 8, 2016, that the 4 BLACKPINK girls officially debuted in public. At that time, even though they had just debuted, BLACKPINK performed impressively and quickly dominated the domestic and foreign market music charts. After the group began to be notified of its activity, all information about the group was searched all over the web. The fan of the 4 girls also gradually increased exponentially.
